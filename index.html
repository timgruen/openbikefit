Welcome to OpenBikeFit

  1. Set up your camera to the side of your bike so your full body is visible
  2. Start pedaling — the system will automatically detect that you've begun and will start measuring
  3. Stop pedaling and come back to the dashboard to see your analysis and fit recommendations

All processing happens on your device. No video is uploaded or stored. This was not created by a professional bike fitter or healthcare professional. Feel free to do your own research and adjust the desired angles with the settings button.
